Key information about the survey:
 
 
 
SURVEY PROVIDER
 
  
 
The survey is being administered externally by an independent consultancy TraQs Consulting and all answers will be treated with the strictest confidence.
 
 
 
CONFIDENTIALITY
 
  
 
PMI will only be able to see aggregate results and any comments you write will not be attributable to you. However, please note that in order to provide actionable feedback for our providers and drive continuous improvement, individual responses regarding third-party services will be shared with the providers (Cartus and KPMG) that PMI has chosen to support you.
 
 
 
TIME NEEDED TO FILL IN THE SURVEY
 
  
 
It should take no more than 20-25 min to fill in the survey. The final duration depends on the additional comments that you may wish to add.
 
 
 
PARTNER SURVEY
 
  
 
The satisfaction of your accompanying partner is a key to the success of the assignment. Therefore, we want to hear from your partners as well. If you are/were accompanied by your partner during an assignment, you can provide his/her name and email address. Your partner will automatically receive an e-mail invitation to a similar survey and will be able to provide the feedback as well.
 
 
 
DEADLINE TO COMPLETE THE SURVEY
 
  
 
The survey will be open for your input from August 2, 2016 (Tuesday) until the end of September 16, 2016 (Friday).
 
 
 
KEY HIGHLIGHTS
 
  
 
After the survey is completed and closed, you will receive a summary of the key highlights and be able to see a comparison between your response to each question and the average response to the same question that all PMI assignees provided.
 
  
 
The first fifty employees to complete a survey will receive a special gift.
